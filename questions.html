Autism Profile Questionnaire

Please answer these questions to help us understand your child's needs better. This information will help us create personalized support through our Autism Allies game.

1. What are your child's biggest daily challenges?
(Identifies priority tasks to gamify—e.g., brushing teeth, transitions, mealtime.)
2. What are your child's favorite interests or motivators?
(Helps embed rewards—e.g., dinosaurs, trains, music, numbers—into the game.)
3. How does your child respond to instructions?
(Verbal, visual, or physical prompts? Do they need step-by-step breakdowns?)
4. What sensory sensitivities impact daily tasks?
(Textures, sounds, or lights that may trigger avoidance—e.g., toothpaste flavors, clothing tags.)
5. What usually helps your child stay calm or focused?
(Deep pressure, fidget tools, quiet time, or a favorite object?)
6. How does your child handle transitions between activities?
(Does a timer, visual schedule, or warning help?)
7. What rewards or reinforcements work best?
(Stickers, screen time, praise, sensory breaks, or tangible rewards?)
8. Does your child understand sequences (e.g., steps to wash hands)?
(May need in-game progress tracking or interactive guides.)
9. What's their attention span for structured tasks?
(Determines game length—short bursts or longer missions?)
10. What's one skill you'd love your child to master independently?
(Focuses the game on a meaningful goal for the family.)
